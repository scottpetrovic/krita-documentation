========
Overview
========

.. image:: /images/en/Krita_Overview_Docker.png

This docker allows you to see a full overview of your image. You can also use it to navigate and zoom in and out quickly.Dragging the view-rectangle allows you quickly move the view. Dragging the zoom-slider allows you quickly change the zoom.
